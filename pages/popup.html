Carleton Lecture Downloader

Automatically downloads the lecture with the highest quality based on debug info in clipboard.

Instructions:

  1. Navigate to the page with the video you want to download.
  2. Right-click on the video player and select Copy debug info.
  3. Open this extension and click Paste & Generate in Auto Mode.
  4. Download the video at your desired quality from the output box.

For more info check out the Github!
